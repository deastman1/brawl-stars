Collabs

Brawl Stars has featured many different collabs through the years. The collabs provide new exclusive skins for brawlers and new game modes to play. Some
events/collabs even had limited time shops with a new currency used to purchase the exclusive things, or just progressive items like coins or new brawlers. The collabs
Brawl Stars has participated in include Spongebob, Godzilla, Toy Story, and many more. All these collabs introduce new skins and ways of progression that continue
to make the game much more engaging.

Godzilla Event

The godzilla event was based on godzilla. The event brought new exclusive skins for brawlers as well as a new game mode. Teams would have to destroy buildings
on eachothers side and the first to destroy all on the opposing side won.

Spongebob

The spongebob event brought new exclusive skins as characters from Spongebob, such as spongebob, plankton, patrick, and mr.krabs. The new game modes was
Jelly fish catching where teams collect jelly fish. A new shop and currency was introduced which were krusty cash. You could buy progressive things as well
as the exclusive skins.

Toy Story

The toy story event brought more exclusive skins from toy story including Woody, Buzz lightyear, and bo peep. The event also brought the first ever unqiue event
limited brawler. The brawler was Buzz Lightyear with 3 modes ehich made him still fun to play. He was free but removed eventually.
